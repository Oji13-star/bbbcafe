The Story

Established in 2025, Bites & Brews & Bouquets was born from a love for warm conversations, handwritten pages, and blooming florals — all served with the perfect cup of coffee. Tucked away in a charming corner, our café blends the comforting hum of a cozy library, the fragrance of handpicked bouquets, and the laughter from a lively table. Whether you're here for a solo journaling session, a friendly game of Scrabble, or a cappuccino date under fairy lights, there's always something brewing for you.

"Bites & Brews & Bouquets isn’t just a café — it’s a curated escape where books bloom, coffee comforts, and every table holds a new story waiting to unfold."
Ojasvi Singh

📚 The Cozy Library

Lose yourself in stories while sipping the perfect latte. Our shelves are filled with timeless classics, hidden gems, and comforting reads — all waiting to be discovered in the warmth of your favorite corner. Join us for intimate literary evenings, poetry readings, and exclusive book signing events with renowned authors.

💐 Floral Boutique

Pick a bloom with your brew. Our handcrafted bouquets are made fresh every day — perfect for brightening your table, gifting a loved one, or simply adding a little beauty to your moment.

☕️ Brew & Bite Bistro

Sip, snack, and savor — From crunchy cookies to warm, fluffy sandwiches — pair your favorites with steamy sips of coffee, tea, or cocoa, each bite is made with comfort and craft in mind.
